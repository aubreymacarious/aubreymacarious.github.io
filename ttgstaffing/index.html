About Us

Join World's Best Marketplace for Workers

Welcome to the Train-to-Go (TTG) Initiative, where we help bridge the gap between talented young Ghanaians and opportunities abroad. Our mission is to provide a platform for young Ghanaians to showcase their abilities and link them to potential job opportunities in the diaspora, particularly in America. Our initiative is born out of the desire to address the growing unemployment and underemployment issues that young Ghanaians face due to the stagnant economy.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. What can I sell?

Be creative! You can offer any service you wish as long as it's legal and complies with our terms. There are over 200 categories you can browse to get ideas.

2. How much money can I make??

It's totally up to you. You can work as much as you want. Many sellers work on TTG full time and some keep their 9-5 job while using TTG to make extra money.

3. How much does it cost?

It's free to join TTG. There is no subscription required or fees to list your services. You keep 80% of each transaction.

4. How much time will I need to invest?

It's very flexible. You need to put in some time and effort in the beginning to learn the marketplace and then you can decide for yourself what amount of work you want to do.

5. How do I price my service?

With Gig Packages, you set your pricing anywhere from $5 - $995 and offer three versions of your service at three different prices.

5. How do I get paid??

Once you complete a buyer's order, the money is transferred to your account. No need to chase clients for payments and wait 60 or 90 days for a check.
